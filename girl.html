You guards are like Death,
bearing your scythes,
and as for my rippling locks,
if you say you'd take them,
then take them!
My hair, once cut, will grow again.
If you cut it again, it will grow again.
When my hair has grown, and you've cut it,
as for the head on which it has grown,
though you think to take it,
you cannot!
